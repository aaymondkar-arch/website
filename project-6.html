Project 06

Urban Artificial Intelligence

Zine · 2025 · Research Methodology

A zine on Urban AI.

What if the city could learn to think? Exploring AI-driven urban systems that sense, adapt, and optimize in real time, and the human responsibility of keeping them in check.

Coupling with parametric modeling environments such as Grasshopper and its analysis plugins like Ladybug, Honeybee, or Karamba enables the simulation and optimization of spatial, environmental, and structural systems. This synthesis allows the urban environment not only to be monitored, but actively reconfigured through intelligent feedback systems shifting toward a cyber-physical ecosystem capable of learning from itself.
