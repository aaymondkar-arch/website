Adaptive Reuse of Paper Mill Buildings

Architectural Design · 2025 · ARCH 700

Adaptive reuse of decommissioned paper mill buildings.

A design studio project examining the transformation of decommissioned paper mill buildings into a new architectural program. The project investigates how industrial heritage structures can absorb contemporary use without erasing the material and spatial history embedded in their fabric.

The design develops a layered intervention strategy: new insertions are legible as contemporary additions while the existing mill structure is repaired and made inhabitable. The work negotiates between preservation and transformation, using the mill's structural logic as both constraint and generative resource.
