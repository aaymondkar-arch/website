Low Cost Housing

Affordable Housing · 2024 · Holyoke, MA

Affordable housing for Holyoke.

A low-cost affordable housing development on an infill site in Holyoke, Massachusetts. Compact housing blocks are organized on a shared grid, then rotated, stacked, and carved - grid, align, volume, fluctuate, voids, final form - so that every unit gets light, air, and outdoor space from a simple, repeatable construction system. The voids left between and within the stacked volumes become balconies, terraces, and planted courts.

At ground level the blocks sit in a continuous shared landscape of paths, gardens, and play spaces, anchored by a community building with dining and common rooms. Rooftop solar arrays offset operating costs, keeping the housing affordable to run as well as to build. Five floor plans document how the units, stairs, and shared spaces stack through the building.
